Inconsistent Manual Inspection

Operator fatigue, shift changes and subjectivity mean the same product receives different pass/fail outcomes. Average consistency score just 2.5 out of 5 in survey responses.

Fragmented Paper Records

Platform Solution

Three Layers, One Integrated Quality System

QC Sentinel combines a physical line-side inspection station, a cloud SaaS dashboard and an operator-centred interface into one unified quality intelligence system built for SME food production environments.

Line-Side Station

Industrial cameras, integrated weigh cell, LED lighting enclosure and edge compute unit. Installed directly on the production line for instant, on-device inspection without connectivity dependency.

Cloud SaaS Platform

Golden Sample Profile Engine, multi-modal quality scoring, batch records database, and drift detection analytics accessible via a secure cloud dashboard from any device.

Operator Interface

Touchscreen pass/warn/fail dashboard for non-technical staff. Role-based access for operators, supervisors, QA leads and management with real-time alerts and one-click audit exports.

Innovation & IP

Five Non-Incremental Innovations With Layered IP Protection

QC Sentinel introduces five core innovations absent from existing prior art: golden sample learning, operator self-calibration, multi-modal fusion, natural-variation envelopes, and production drift detection.

Innovation Radar vs Prior ArtQC Sentinel Differentiation

Five Core Innovation Pillars

Golden Sample Profile Engine
Learns acceptable variation from real approved samples instead of fixed engineering templates. Handles natural inconsistency in bakery, pastries and handmade foods.
Operator-Trained Self-Calibration
Non-technical factory staff train and update inspection logic on the production floor, no machine-vision engineers or coding required at any stage.
Multi-Modal Quality Signature
Weight, shape, colour, surface and packaging fused into a single quality decision, not isolated pass/fail checks that miss holistic defects.
Production Drift Detection
Periodic sampling longitudinally identifies gradual deterioration before large batches fail, turning reactive inspection into predictive quality management.
Natural-Variation Tolerance Envelope
Explicitly models acceptable artisan variability. The system tolerates irregular crusts and browning differences while detecting genuine manufacturing defects.

Frequently Asked Questions

What food products does QC Sentinel support?
+
QC Sentinel is designed for naturally variable and semi-manual food products: bakery goods, pastries, muffins, flatbreads, frozen snacks, chilled prepared foods and artisan handmade products. It handles natural variation in shape, colour and texture that traditional machine-vision systems cannot accommodate.
Do our operators need technical training?
+
No specialist technical training is required. The platform is designed for non-technical frontline staff. Operators can train golden sample profiles, update tolerances and perform inspections through a simple touchscreen interface. Calibration takes minutes, not days.
How does the pilot programme work?
+
We install a QC Sentinel station on one production line at your facility for a structured 30-90 day pilot. We provide full onboarding, golden sample profiling and operator training at no additional cost during the pilot. Commercial contract offered on successful completion.
How does QC Sentinel handle product recipe changes?
+
The operator self-calibration system allows any authorised team member to create a new golden sample profile for a revised recipe in minutes. The system then learns the new acceptable variation envelope without requiring any engineering support or software update.
